Edgar Allan Poe

Poetry and terror in one person.

Edgar Allan Poe timeline

  • Jan 1809 - Born in Boston, Massachusetts.
  • Feb 1826 - Attends the University of Virginia.
  • May 1827 - Joins US Army.
  • Jul 1830 - Enrolls at West Point.
  • Feb 1831 - Dismissed from West Point.
  • Oct 1833 - After winning a contest Edgar Allan Poe's "MS. Found in a Bottle" is published.
  • Sep 1835 - Edgar Allan Poe marries his cousin, Virginia Clemm.
  • Dec 1835 - Takes a Job as Editor of the Southern Literary Messenger.
  • Jul 1838 - Publishes First Novel "The Narrative of Arthur Gordon Pym".
  • Nan 1839 - "Tales of the Grotesque and Arabesque" Edgar Allan Poe's first volume of short stories is published.
  • Jul 1839 - Becomes Assistant Editor of Burton's Gentleman's Magazine.
  • Feb 1841 - Becomes the Editor at Graham's Magazine.
  • Apr 1844 - Moves to New York and Works for the New York Evening Mirror.
  • Nov 1844 - "Thou Art The Man" Is Published.
  • Jan 1845 - "The Raven" is published
  • Jan 1847 - Virginia Clemm Poe Dies.
  • Nov 1847 - Proposes to Poet Sarah Whitman but Engagement is Called Off.
  • Aug 1849 1831 - Becomes Engaged to Sarah Elmira Royster.
  • Oct 1849 - Edgar Allan Poe dies.
